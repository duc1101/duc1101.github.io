Hospitalization procedure


After you have agreed to use medical services, please complete the hospital fee advance when you are admitted to the hospital at the nearest cashier.

  • Surgical services: Advance of 100% of surgical service fees.
  • Inpatient service: Advance 100% of the amount according to the doctor's cost estimate before entering hospital and hospital regulations.

Administrative documents upon admission

You need to show the following documents at the Cashier or Internal Nursing Counter in Within 24 hours after being admitted to the hospital to solve problems related to insurance and administrative procedures other:

  • Health Insurance card (if any), Private insurance card (required within 24 hours from the time of admission - if).
  • Identity card / Passport, Birth certificate (if children).
  • Other medical records required by the doctor (tests, imaging results, prescriptions ...)

    • Note on admission:

    • You should refrain from bringing valuable objects into the hospital, and preserve your own property During the time of using the hospital.
    • Hospitalized women need to prepare a set of clothes for women and a warm towel for babies when they leave the hospital.
    • You do not bring any extra food, drink, fruit or fresh flowers into the hospital.

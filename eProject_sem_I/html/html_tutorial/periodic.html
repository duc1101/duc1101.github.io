Instructions for using the software to connect online with doctors when participating in health care programs healthy from afar

The Distance Health Program at Vinmec Times City Hospital and Vinmec Central Park is the award new measures, meeting the health care needs of people in the context of the Covid-19 epidemic complicated movements. When participating in the Program, customers do not need to go to the hospital and still can Direct interaction when needed with doctors, nursing through specialized support tools, guaranteed quality and effectiveness of medical examination and treatment.

Customers will receive a SMS (SMS) confirming successful appointment and a link (link) to post enter into the Theranest software. This is the software used to connect online with the doctors in Distance Health Care Program

The link can be opened directly on smartphones (using a browser to open websites), devices tablet, laptop or desktop computer. Before the appointment, please check the status Connect to the network to make sure your device can access the software.

The following is the detailed Theranest software user guide:

Step 1:

Click on the link (link) of the software that has been sent via SMS (SMS), Select Allow / Allow Position 1) to activate your microphone and camera. When the device is ready, customers can immediately see the image on their side as the illustration below.

Step 2:

After installing the image and sound, please enter the customer name (Position No. 2) and click the Join button Join "Join Vinmec Healthcare System"

Step 3:

Instructions for making authorization to duplicate Medical Records

For your convenience, there is a need to copy medical records in case of not coming The hospital that authorizes another person to come. Below, Vinmec International General Hospital for guidance perform authorization to duplicate Medical Records.

Details are as follows:

  • Step 1: Fill out the power of attorney form, sign for confirmation, and certify the relative country competent (Ward, commune)
  • Step 2: Authorized person to fill out the Application form for medical record, contents need stars y
  • Step 3: Bring the authorization letter and the medical record application form to the hospital reception Vinmec to be instructed

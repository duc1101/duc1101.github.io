A guide for customers with Health Insurance

Step 1:
  • If you are a new customer: You need to fill in the Registration Form at the Registration Counter.
  • For old customers: Customers do not have to fill out the Registration Form.
Step 2:
  • Customers examine and treat with doctors and pay all hospital fees incurred at the Cashier of examination departments.
  • Customers receive prescription drugs (if any) from the hospital pharmacy.
Step 3:

When making payment, the customer presents the following documents:

Correct route
  • The health insurance card is still valid.
  • ID card or other valid photo ID or Birth certificate (for children).
  • ID card or other valid photo ID or Birth certificate (for children).
  • If the health insurance card does not have registration for initial medical examination and treatment at Vinmec Hospital, a paper certificate is required transfer patients to Health Insurance according to form 02 / BYT.
Offline:
  • The health insurance card is still valid.
  • Identity card, or valid photo ID, or Birth Certificate (for children).
  • Paper of hospital transfer according to the form of the Ministry of Health. If transferred via an intermediate route (Example: Tu Huyen »Province» Vinmec), The customer needs a photocopy of the original referral to the intermediary (Paper of transfer from district to province).
  • List of service charges and Prescriptions (if any).
Step 4:
  • Customers receive the hospital fee back under the Social Insurance (Social Insurance) regime and pay the amount
  • The drug is outside the scope of payment of social insurance (if any) at the hospital's Insurance Counter.

Maternity Services and Childbirth Package

The happiest of parents and family is to see the baby born. To help mom The pregnancy has a healthy pregnancy and the birth process is carried out safely and smoothly, Da Hospital The International Cambridge Hospital Department has developed a package of maternity services to "Accompany with mother, ready to pick up babies". Certainly, the mother and family will have the best and most satisfying experience with the utmost care The professional and professional team of doctors and nurses at the hospital.

Prenatal care

  • Physical examination
  • Physical examination
  • Prenatal care
  • Examination with anesthesiologist
  • Ultrasonic transducers
  • Fetal ultrasound (2D & 5D)
  • Monitoring fetal heart and uterine contractions
  • Routine blood test
  • Blood group analysis of ABO, RH
  • Total analysis of peripheral blood TB
  • Total urinalysis (By automatic device)
  • Early detection of abnormalities of fetal down syndrome - Double test
  • HBs Ag, HIV Ab, Syphilis rapid test
  • Testing for antibodies to hepatitis B, syphilis and Rubbella (rapid test)
  • Get tested for Streptococcus B bacteria in the 8th month of pregnancy

In birth

  • Give birth or caesarean section
  • Pain relief procedures during cesarean delivery (spinal anesthesia)
  • Esthetic surgery / perineal floor
  • Skin to skin

    • Postpartum care

    • Give vitamin K to the baby
    • Get vaccinated against hepatitis B for babies
    • Meals, utensils (clothes, diapers, milk ...) for mom and baby
    • Newborn checkups for children
    • Special care for mother and baby.

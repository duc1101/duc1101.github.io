Kinh nghiệm đi khám

Usually, cardiologists will only examine in the morning at Clinic 1 and not the day Every appointment has a schedule. Therefore, patients should go to the clinic early to catch up and read the results within morning.

Patients visit the website of Hanoi Medical University Hospital to look up the weekly schedule. Besides, You can also call the hospital hotline 19006422 to ask for a schedule and book a day in advance.

When booking in advance, patients will be scheduled to come for examination, but still take time to wait examination. However, it will also save a lot of time waiting for a visit.

Note that when the patient is examined, it is advisable to go straight to the clinic entrance located separate from the gate Medical University Hospital (Hanoi Medical University is located in the middle of Medical University Hospital and Clinic No. 1). IN There is space for motorbikes and cars for patients.
